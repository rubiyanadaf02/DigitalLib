Sweeter with Strawberries

STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE CRUMBLE BARS Makes 12 bars With a buttery shortbread base, a light cream cheese filling folded with whipped cream and packed with chopped fresh strawberries, and a sprinkling of crisp shortbread crumbles and freeze-dried strawberries, this shareable shortcake is sure to brighten up anyone's day. Bursting with strawberry flavor, these bars are the perfect springtime dessert. 1 cup (227 grams) unsalted butter, softened 1/3 cup (73 grams) firmly packed light brown sugar 1 large egg (50 grams), room temperature 31½ cups (406 grams) all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon (3 grams) kosher salt 1 teaspoon (4 grams) vanilla extract 8 ounces (226 grams) cream cheese, softened 1 cup (120 grams) confectioners' sugar 1 cup (240 grams) heavy whipping cream 3/4 cup (140 grams) chopped fresh strawberries 1/4 cup (7 grams) crushed freeze-dried strawberries.

Nutty Choco Delux

PANETTONE COOKIES Makes about 20 cookies These tender treats capture the essence of panettone, the Italian holiday sweet bread. Take part in this delightful twist on an Italian classic by blending candied fruits and nuts-essential to the traditional bread-into a rich, buttery cookie dough that bakes to golden perfection. A dip in white chocolate adds a special touch for the holidays. 3/4 cup (170 grams) unsalted butter, softened 2/3 cup (133 grams) granulated sugar 1½ teaspoon (15 grams) kosher salt 1/2 teaspoon (2 grams) vanilla extract 13/4 cups (219 grams) all-purpose flour 1/3 cup (42 grams) peeled roasted chestnuts, chopped 1/4 cup (32 grams) golden raisins, chopped 1/4 cup (45 grams) chopped candied orange peel, plus more for garnish 1/4 cup (32 grams) chopped dried cherries 8 ounces (226 grams) white chocolate, chopped 1 tablespoon (14 grams) coconut oil Garnish: toasted sliced almonds

Better with Butter

MAILÄNDERLI Makes about 22 cookies Recipe and Food Styling by Andie Pilot Photo by Joann Pai These are the standard Swiss Christmas cookies made by nearly every bakery and every family in the country. Buttery, with a hint of lemon, the taste is a nostalgic reminder of childhood and Christmas for Swiss people throughout the world. Dough: 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon (127 grams) unsalted butter, room temperature 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons (124 grams) granulated sugar 3 large egg yolks (56 grams), room temperature 1 teaspoon (2 grams) lemon zest 1½ teaspoon kosher salt 2 cups (250 grams) all-purpose flour, sifted Glaze: 1 large egg yolk (19 grams), room temperature 1½ teaspoon granulated sugar
